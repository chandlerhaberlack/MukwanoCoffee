Our Story

Mukwano Coffee started in Casper, Wyoming, born from friendships in Uganda and a desire to support the families and communities behind our beans. Now operated in Rawlins by Cody and Christine Anderson, we offer single origin, specialty-grade coffee—roasted to highlight each bean’s unique character.

"Mukwano gwange means 'my friend' in Luganda."
